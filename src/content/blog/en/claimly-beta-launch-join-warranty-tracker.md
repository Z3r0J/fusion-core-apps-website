## What Is Claimly?

Claimly is a **personal inventory and warranty tracker** that keeps all your purchases, receipts, and warranty details in one organized place. Instead of keeping paper receipts in a drawer or relying on memory, Claimly stores everything digitally — with automatic expiry alerts so you never miss a claim window again.

Think of it as a warranty book for the 21st century.

### Scan Any Receipt in Seconds

The centerpiece of Claimly is its **AI-powered receipt scanner**. Open the app, point your camera at any receipt, and Claimly automatically extracts:

- Product name
- Store name
- Purchase date
- Total price

No manual entry. The AI reads the receipt and pre-fills everything. You review, confirm, and save — done.

### Track Every Type of Warranty

Most products come with multiple warranty layers that are easy to forget:

- **Store warranty** — the retailer's return or exchange period
- **Manufacturer warranty** — the brand's coverage, usually 1-2 years
- **Extended warranty** — any additional coverage you purchased separately

Claimly tracks all three per product, with individual expiry dates and countdown timers. You see exactly how many days remain on each coverage type, right from the product detail screen.

### Get Alerted Before It Is Too Late

Claimly sends you a notification before warranties expire so you have time to act. No more discovering that your laptop warranty lapsed three weeks ago.

### Store Everything in One Place

Beyond warranties, Claimly is a full **product inventory**. Each item can have:

- A photo of the product
- Scanned or uploaded PDF receipts
- Multiple warranty entries
- Category tags (Electronics, Hogar, Electrodomésticos, and more)

## FAQ

### Is Claimly free?

Yes. Claimly is free to download and use. It contains ads to support development, but the core features — scanning, tracking, and alerts — are all available without any payment.

### Do I need an account to use Claimly?

No account is required to add products and track warranties. Your data is stored locally on your device.

### Why do I need to join a Google Group to download it?

Google requires apps to complete a closed testing phase before going public. During this phase, only approved testers can install the app. Joining the Google Group is how you get approved. This is temporary — once testing is complete, Claimly will be available on Google Play without this step.

### What types of receipts can Claimly scan?

Claimly can scan printed receipts, handwritten receipts, and printed invoices. The AI works best with clearly legible text and good lighting. You can also manually enter details or upload a PDF if you prefer.

### Will my data be lost after beta ends?

No. The app and your data carry over seamlessly from beta to the production release.
