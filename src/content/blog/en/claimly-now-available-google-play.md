## The Problem Nobody Talks About

You buy a laptop. It comes with a one-year manufacturer warranty, a 90-day store return policy, and an extended protection plan you paid extra for. You put the receipt in a drawer and tell yourself you will organize it later.

Eighteen months pass. The laptop stops charging. You dig through that drawer and find nothing.

This happens constantly — and it costs people real money. The warranty was valid. The product was defective. But without proof of purchase and a record of the coverage dates, there is nothing you can do.

Existing warranty tracker apps on Google Play have not solved this problem well. Most require manual data entry, limit you to one warranty per product, and have not been meaningfully updated in years. The #1 complaint across competitor reviews is always the same: you cannot track multiple warranties on a single item.

That is exactly the gap Claimly fills.

## Key Features

### OCR Receipt Scanning

Point your camera at any printed receipt and Claimly does the rest. The on-device AI reads the receipt and automatically extracts:

- Product name
- Store name
- Purchase date
- Total amount

The form pre-fills instantly. You review, adjust anything that needs correcting, and save. No typing required. An internet connection is required for OCR scanning.

The starter plan includes 5 OCR scans per month. Premium unlocks unlimited scanning.

### Multiple Warranties Per Product

This is the feature that makes Claimly different from every other warranty tracker app on Google Play.

Most products come with layered coverage that is easy to lose track of:

| Warranty Type | What It Covers |
|---|---|
| **Manufacturer warranty** | Defects from the brand, typically 1–2 years |
| **Store warranty** | Return or exchange period from the retailer |
| **Extended coverage** | Any protection plan you purchased separately |

Other warranty apps force you to pick one. Claimly tracks all three on a single product entry, each with its own start date, end date, and countdown timer.

Status badges use color to tell you what is happening at a glance — green for active coverage with plenty of time, amber for warranties expiring soon, and red for anything already expired.

### Smart Expiration Alerts

Claimly monitors your warranties in the background and sends push notifications automatically:

- **30 days before expiry** — enough time to decide whether to renew or file a claim
- **7 days before expiry** — a clear reminder to act
- **1 day before expiry** — final warning before coverage ends

The home screen **Priority Dashboard** shows your most time-sensitive products at the top with a direct action button. You never need to remember to check — Claimly tells you when something needs attention.

Premium users can customize the notification schedule to match how far in advance they prefer to be reminded.

### Product Inventory with Attachments

Each product entry is a complete file. Attach:

- Product photos
- Scanned receipts or uploaded PDF invoices
- Warranty card photos
- PDF manuals or service agreements

Everything you would need to file a claim is in one place, accessible in a single tap. Products are organized by categories — Electronics, Home, Appliances, Clothing, Vehicles, and more — and the full inventory is searchable and filterable.

### Privacy-First

Claimly requires no account. Your data stays on your device — no cloud sync, no sign-in, no personal information collected beyond what you enter yourself. OCR scanning requires an internet connection; all other features (browsing your inventory, managing warranties, viewing attachments) work fully offline.

The only permission Claimly requires beyond standard storage is camera access — and only when you choose to scan a receipt.

### Dark Mode by Default

Claimly ships with a modern dark mode UI as the default experience, with color-coded status badges that are easy to read in any lighting condition.
