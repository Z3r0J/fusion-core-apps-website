## CartWise Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 26, 2026

Thank you for using **CartWise**, developed by **FusionCore Apps** ("we", "our", "us").
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use CartWise — a grocery and shopping-list app. CartWise is designed with privacy in mind: **no name, email, or password is ever required or collected.** However, to operate shared lists and manage subscriptions, the app generates a random **anonymous account identifier** (an anonymous user ID via our backend authentication service, Supabase) that is not linked to your personal identity. This identifier is processed as described below.

---

## 1. Introduction

CartWise is a shopping-list app built by **FusionCore Apps**. Its core features — creating lists, adding items, organizing by category, and tracking your budget — work entirely offline and store all data locally on your device. Optional features such as shared lists and push notifications involve minimal data processed on our backend, as described below. We are committed to collecting only what is necessary to operate these features and nothing more.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Data Stored Only on Your Device

The following data is created and stored exclusively on your device (using SQLite and encrypted local storage) and is **never transmitted to our servers**:

- Your shopping lists, items, categories, and all related content (quantities, units, prices, notes, checked state)
- App preferences: theme, font, language, notification settings

### Shared-List Data (Only When You Create or Join a Shared List)

If you choose to create or join a shared list, the following information is stored on our backend (Supabase) to keep the list in sync across all members:

- The list and item content (names, quantities, units, prices, notes, checked state)
- A **randomly generated anonymous member ID** and an **emoji avatar** assigned to you — no name, email, or personal identity is attached
- An **anonymous account identifier** generated by our backend authentication (Supabase Auth) to uniquely identify your session without tying it to any personal identity
- Your **Expo push notification token** (if you have notifications enabled), used to alert you to shared-list activity

You are never required to provide a name, email address, or any identifying information to use shared lists.

### Push Notification Data (Only If You Opt In)

If you grant notification permission, we store:

- Your **Expo push token** and device locale (language)
- Your notification preferences: frequency, preferred notification time, timezone, and quiet-hours settings

This data is used solely to deliver shared-list activity alerts, list reminders, and re-engagement notices you have opted into. Your push token is **automatically removed** from our system when notification delivery fails (which typically indicates the app has been uninstalled) and is **soft-deleted** when you turn off notifications in-app.

### Voice Input

With microphone permission, CartWise lets you add items by voice. Speech-to-text conversion is performed **entirely on your device** by your operating system's native speech engine. **No audio is uploaded to CartWise servers** — only the recognized text is used to create a list item.

### Camera

The camera is used **only** to scan QR codes for joining a shared list. No images are captured, stored, or transmitted.

### Subscription Data

If you purchase **CartWise Pro**, the transaction is processed by **RevenueCat** and the **Google Play Store**. These services use a stable, anonymous app-user ID and your entitlement/purchase status to grant Pro access. We **never receive or store your payment-card details**.

### IP Address & Backend Requests

When the app contacts our backend (Supabase edge functions) for shared-list sync, push token registration, or subscription verification, our servers may transiently process your device's **IP address** for security, abuse prevention, and rate limiting. This IP address is not stored long-term alongside your list data and is not used for advertising. Supabase's infrastructure handles this processing; see the [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy) for details.

### Advertising Data (Free Tier Only)

The free version of CartWise may show ads served by **Google AdMob**. With your consent (collected via a Google UMP consent prompt), AdMob may independently collect:

- Advertising identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
- Approximate location (for region-based ad targeting)
- Device and usage signals (e.g., device model, engagement with ads)

We do not control what AdMob collects directly; please review their policies (see Section 4).

### Analytics & Crash Data

**Firebase Analytics** and **Firebase Crashlytics** collect limited usage and diagnostic data — which may include device and app identifiers and event data — to help us understand which features are used and fix crashes. This data is collected via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics and is not linked to your name or email, but may include pseudonymous device-level identifiers as determined by Google's Firebase platform.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data described above only to:

- **Sync shared lists** across members in real time
- **Deliver push notifications** you have opted into
- **Manage Pro entitlements** and verify your subscription status
- **Show ads** to free-tier users (subject to your consent)
- **Improve app stability and features** via aggregated analytics and crash reports

We do **not** sell or rent your data to any third party.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services We Share Data With

We share the minimum necessary data with the following service providers, solely so they can perform their respective functions:

- **Supabase** — backend infrastructure for shared-list sync, storage, and anonymous authentication. Supabase may also transiently process your IP address for security and rate limiting when the app contacts our edge functions. [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)
- **Expo Push Service & Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)** — notification delivery infrastructure. [Firebase Privacy & Security](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **Google AdMob** — ad serving and measurement (free tier only, with your consent):
  - [Google AdMob Program Policies](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)
  - [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  - [Google Ads Technologies](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)
- **RevenueCat & Google Play Billing** — subscription management and entitlement verification:
  - [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
  - [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics** — usage and diagnostic data collection (may include device and app identifiers). [Firebase Privacy & Security](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

These providers use the data **solely to perform their service**. None of them receive data for advertising or sale on our behalf, and we do not sell your data to any party.

---

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion

- **Local data** (lists, items, preferences) is removed from your device when you uninstall the app.
- **Push tokens** are automatically removed from our system on delivery failure (typically at uninstall) and are soft-deleted when you opt out of notifications in-app.
- **Shared-list data** persists on our backend to keep the list available to other members. If you wish to have your shared-list data removed, contact us (see Section 11) and we will process the deletion request.

---

## 6. Data Security

We protect your data through:

- **Encrypted on-device storage** for local lists and preferences
- **Encryption in transit** (TLS) for all communication with our backend
- **Supabase Row-Level Security (RLS)** to ensure only authorized members can access a shared list

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We apply reasonable safeguards and will notify affected users of any breach as required by applicable law.

---

## 7. Your Rights & Choices

You can exercise the following controls at any time:

- **Microphone & Camera**: Revoke these permissions in your device's system settings.
- **Notifications**: Turn off notifications in-app or revoke notification permission in system settings. Your push token will be removed from our system.
- **Ad personalization**: Opt out via your device's "Ads" / "Ad personalization" settings and your Google account's Ads Settings.
- **Data deletion**: Email us at **support@fusioncoreapps.com** to request deletion of any data we hold (e.g., shared-list data, push tokens).

If you are located in the **European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom**, you may also have the right to access, correct, object to, or restrict processing of your personal data under the GDPR or UK GDPR. If you are a **California resident**, you may have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information). Contact us to exercise any of these rights.

## 8. Children's Privacy

CartWise is intended for users aged **13 and older**. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided data through our app, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

## 9. International Data Transfers

CartWise is developed and operated globally. Data processed on our backend (Supabase) and by our service providers (Firebase, RevenueCat, AdMob) may be stored and processed on servers located outside your country of residence. By using CartWise, you consent to such international transfers. We rely on our service providers' compliance frameworks (including Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable) to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.
