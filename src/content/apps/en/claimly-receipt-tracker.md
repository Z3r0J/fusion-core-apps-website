## Overview
**Claimly: Warranty & Receipt Tracker** is your personal inventory for everything you own. Scan receipts with your camera, let AI auto-extract the product name, store, date, and price, then track store warranties, manufacturer warranties, and extended warranties — all with countdown timers so you never miss a claim deadline.

## Features
- **Receipt scanner** — point your camera at any receipt, AI extracts all the details automatically
- **Warranty tracking** — track store, manufacturer, and extended warranties per product
- **Expiry alerts** — get notified before warranties expire so you can claim in time
- **High-priority dashboard** — see what's expiring soon at a glance
- **Product inventory** — organize everything you own with photos, receipts, and documents attached
- **Multiple warranty types** — store, manufacturer, and extended warranties per item
- **Attachment storage** — attach PDFs, photos, and documents to each product
- **Category organization** — Electronics, Hogar, Electrodomésticos, and more

## Perfect for
Anyone who has ever missed a warranty claim, lost a receipt, or forgotten when they bought something. Claimly is your digital warranty book.
