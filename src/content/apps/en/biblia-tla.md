## Overview
**Biblia TLA — Spanish Bible App** brings the *Traducción en Lenguaje Actual* (TLA) to Android — a clear, modern Spanish translation designed to make Scripture accessible to readers of all ages. With built-in **Audio Bible**, **full offline access**, **daily verses**, and **weekly reading plans**, it's your daily companion for spiritual growth in Spanish.

## Features
- **Complete TLA Bible** — modern, natural Spanish that stays faithful to the original message
- **Integrated Audio Bible** — listen while commuting, walking, or resting
- **100% offline access** — read anywhere without an internet connection
- **Daily verses** — personalized inspiration to share with family and friends
- **Weekly reading plans** — downloadable guides to maintain a consistent reading habit
- **Highlights & favorites** — save and revisit your most meaningful passages
- **Quick search** — find texts and keywords instantly
- **Intuitive navigation** — jump to any book, chapter, and verse in seconds
- **Personalized reading** — dark/light mode, font size, fonts, and color themes

## Perfect for
Daily devotions in Spanish, Bible study for all ages, and anyone looking for a **Spanish Bible with audio and offline** for home, work, or travel.

The **TLA translation** stands out for its accessible, everyday language. With **integrated audio**, **daily verses**, **weekly reading plans**, and **offline mode**, this app makes it easy to build a consistent spiritual habit.
