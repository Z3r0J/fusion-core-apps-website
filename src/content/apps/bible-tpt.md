## Overview
**Bible TPT: Passion Translation** is a modern **TPT Bible app** designed to help you read and listen to **The Passion Translation (TPT)** with a clean, distraction-free experience. Stay connected to Scripture anywhere with **offline Bible access**, **Audio Bible**, **Daily Verse**, and **weekly Reading Plans** that keep you consistent.

## Features
- **TPT Bible (The Passion Translation)** with fast, easy navigation
- **Audio Bible** for hands-free listening (commute, workout, reflection)
- **Offline Bible access** — read without an internet connection
- **Daily Verse** for daily inspiration and consistent engagement
- **Weekly Reading Plans** to guide your Bible reading routine
- **Bookmarks & Highlights** to save and revisit meaningful passages
- **Personalized reading experience** (themes, font size, brightness)
- **Easy verse sharing** to inspire friends and family

## Perfect for
Daily devotions, Bible study, travel, and anyone who wants the **TPT Bible offline** with **Audio Bible** and tools that make reading simpler and more consistent.

## Why choose Bible TPT?
**The Passion Translation** uses expressive, heartfelt language that helps Scripture feel vivid and personal. With offline access, audio, and daily content, Bible TPT is built for believers who want a practical, modern Bible app for everyday spiritual growth.
