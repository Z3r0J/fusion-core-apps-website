Features

Everything your grocery trip needs

Built for real shopping — not for demos.

Real-time Price Calculator

See the running total update as you tick items. Know your budget before you reach the checkout.

Voice-to-Text Input

Say "2 liters of milk" and it lands on your list. Hands full? Voice works.

AI-Generated Lists

Tell CartWise what you're cooking and it builds the ingredient list for you.

Instant Sharing

Share via QR code or WhatsApp link. Everyone sees updates in real time, no account needed.

Budget Tracker & Analytics

Spending reports by category let you spot where the budget is going each week.

Smart Auto-Categorization

Items are grouped by aisle automatically, so you move through the store in order.

Smart Reminders

Set a reminder to leave the house at the right time — CartWise pings you, not the other way around.

Offline-First

All your lists are stored locally. No signal in the store? CartWise still works perfectly.
